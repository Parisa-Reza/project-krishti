Why Krishti?


Bangladesh is mainly a ruler country having old and rich cultural heritage. In ruler area, hereditary culture practice can be seen. They make authentic world class crafts and products which bears our Bengali custom.

Our website is a online market platform dedicated to these underrated talents. Here people can select ,add into the cart and buy these things. Offers and sale will be available in this platform In our website our cultural products like earthenware , pottery, Nakshi Kantha, authentic jamdani, moslin sharee, bamboo made items, fashion and home decoration accessories with a fusion of Bengali custom, extremely fresh and authentic food items like ‘Ghee’,’Kashundi’,’Amshotto’, mustard oil, pickles etc.There will be category of sections of products. A review section will also be included. These traditional expertism, cultural aestheticism needs more attention and market value.

This website will present these products to the consumesr not only for practicing our culture but also use our home products which can decline the dependency of imported products. Moreover, the more the advertisement is, the more the employment and livelihood of ruler people will increase. This may also spread our culture globally and get worldwide recognition. Lastly the most important thing is this platform will prevent the extinguishment of our Bengali custom in this digitalized and western fused generation.
